## Privacy Policy

If you send a text message to this number, I may receive and process
your phone number, message content, and basic message metadata to relay
the message, create alerts or incidents, and operate this contact
channel. I may use third-party providers, including Twilio and
PagerDuty, to receive, transmit, log, and process that information.
Message data may be retained by me or those providers as needed for
operation, security, troubleshooting, and related recordkeeping.

## Terms & Conditions

By sending a message to this number, you agree to these Terms &
Conditions and to the processing of your information as described in the
Privacy Policy. This number is a personal/urgent contact channel only;
texting it does not create a customer, business, fiduciary, or
confidential relationship. Messages may not be monitored at all times,
and no response, action, or delivery outcome is guaranteed, so do not
use this number for emergencies or other time-sensitive situations where
delay or failure could cause harm. Please do not send passwords,
financial information, medical information, or other highly sensitive
content. This service is provided "as is" and may be changed, limited,
or discontinued at any time.
